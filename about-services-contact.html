Meet
Kimberly

As a photographer and graphic artist, I’ve always loved art. My first passion was drawing—capturing the gaze in people’s eyes as they looked at something beautiful, the relaxed placement of their hands, and the subtle curve of their mouths when someone they loved entered the room. I found joy in painting, too, with its vibrant colors, landscapes, and cloudscapes. Photography, for me, became an extension of this love. It allows me to capture people in ways they might not see themselves.

I began photographing brides because I admire the timeless black-and-white family portraits. I love capturing the beauty and significance of those moments, while creating a visual stories, unique paintings made of special moments.

"A man can wear a tux any time, but a woman wears her wedding dress only once. The dress is a symbol of the wedding itself."

The playfulness and creativity that clients bring to a photoshoot are what I love most. It’s a collaboration, an artistic partnership, where we create together, capturing not just a pose but an experience, a feeling.

Each photograph is a window, revealing a unique essence, a fleeting expression, or a connection. It’s a moment preserved, forever timeless.
